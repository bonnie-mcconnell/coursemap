How to use CourseMap

⚠️ Unofficial tool - not affiliated with Massey University. Always verify with official Massey programme info and your academic advisor.

Getting started

  1. Type your major name and select from the dropdown
  2. Set your campus, delivery mode, and start semester
  3. Optionally add a minor or double major
  4. Add completed courses so the planner skips them
  5. Click Generate plan

Known limitations

  • Prerequisites - ~64% of L200+ courses have incomplete or inferred prerequisite data. Always check prerequisites manually before enrolling.
  • Courses marked ? - 323 courses have no confirmed 2026 offering data. Verify availability at Massey.
  • Minors (beta) - Minor scheduling is approximate. Check massey.ac.nz/study/minors for official requirements.
  • Fee estimates - Rough 2026 approximations only. Use massey.ac.nz/fees for exact amounts.
  • Elective restrictions - Some programmes say "choose from List A only" - the planner doesn't enforce these.

Before enrolling

  1. Verify programme requirements at massey.ac.nz/study
  2. Check prerequisites for every L200+ course
  3. Confirm course availability for your enrolment year
  4. Verify any minor requirements separately
  5. Book with your academic advisor
